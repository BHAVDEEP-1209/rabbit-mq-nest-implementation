Broker working
1. exchange -> direct, fanout, topic
2. Queues tied to exchange
3. messages goes to Queues through exchanges
4.consumers subscribe to Queues
5. after the message is consumed and is acknowlegded by consumer to rabbit mq, it is deleted from the queue (acknowledgement)
6.If there is no acknowledgement after set time (default 30 mins), the consumer connection is closed and the 'UNACKNOWLEDGED' messages are sent back to the queue

Note: Acknowledgement must be sent to same channel that received the message

Messages are distributed to different consumers in round-robin fashion
