Choreography 

simple generation of event by micro service to be used by other micro service and so on to complete a business process 

Orchestration 

centralized control of business process so that compensatory actions (backward compatibility) is ensured

Saga is architecture for implementing Distributes transaction across multiple systems

Saga can be implemented in two ways

Saga Orchestration (centralized control)

Simple implementation
Tight occupling on orchestrator
Easy to debug and track
High risk of failure if orchestrator fails

Saga Choreography (decentralized control)
Service interact with each other independently by publishing and consuming events also performing compenstory actions by themselves 
No centralized control
Loose coupling
Harder to debug and track
Low risk of failure if one service fails
